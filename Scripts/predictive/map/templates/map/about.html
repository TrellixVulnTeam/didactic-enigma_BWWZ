Epidemics

Hailing from different corners of the country, our team, Predictive26, is overwhelmingly diverse, not only geographically, but also in our areas of expertise. With one mechanical engineer, two advanced coders, a business and design major, and two chemical engineers, the average day for us is not unlike something you might see on television. With our wide range of skills and creative insights constantly flowing, we looked at every angle possible to try and come up with the best solution for our problem. And our result, HealthRAIS, is the system that all of this is devoted to.
